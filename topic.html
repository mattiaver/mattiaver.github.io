Why is blockchain invented?

For that we need to look at cash money. Every dollar is marked by a centralized authority. This makes it possible for an authority to keep track of everything. This means that the authority has also all the power, because they can keep track of the records. If they want to print extra money, they can just do it without anyone knowing it. This is the reason Bitcoin was born. It removes the need of a centralized authority, because everyone can keep track of it. Decentralization reduces the risk of fraud. Bitcoin applies the block chain technology. This technology has four important elements:

  • It needs a peer to peer network: open for anyone and everyone.
  • Cryptography: this one is important for the safety. If everyone can particpate, also bad actor can do it.
  • Consensus algorithm: Not everyone can just add a block to the blockchain. First there needs to be a proof of work. Only if the computer solves a math problem first, it is allowed to add a block to a blockchain.
  • The last element is punishment and reward: everytime someone writes a new page, a reward is given as coins.

Transactions and security

It would take a long time if every single peer needs to approve the transaction. How it works is that if the transaction gets approved by 51 procent of the peers, then it is considered final. Suppose a malicious person wants to add or change a transaction, then he will have to obtain more than 50 percent of the computing power of the blockchain network. The more people are connected to the blockchain network, the safer the transactions are stored.
